Who is it?

Its main headquarters is located in California and has small firms located throughout the United States and Britain. It was created by John Warner and his brother Jacob Warner 500 years ago in the United States.

Goals

Their goal is to film a real live movie in outerspace so that people won't think that all they do is film things by doing special effects. They also have the goal of filming a real live space battle in the future when they have enough money for the props for that battle.

The thought of keeping order through movies and the tickets off of the planet if things happen is the reasons why their employees going to work everyday.
