NASA is a federal government agency of the United States

Quote

Who is it?

Its main headquarters is located somewhere in Alaska and it has multiple branches throughout the United States and countries allied with the United States. It was started up 600 years ago when Space became something that can be reached by man.

Goals

Their goal is to promote space travel in order to reduce the population by reducing the numbers of Humans on Earth. In order to do so, they would need to find habitatable planets for humans to colonize.

Attitude

Right now, the tensions on Earth is increasing due to the decrease of fresh water supplies and the decrease of food. They have already sent out colonization teams to colonize closer planets but those planets are not enough to satisfy the population growths and the decreasing number of food supplies. They are desperate and are partnering with anyone capable of sending out spaceships to explore the unknown.

Behavior

NASA uses all of the social media sites that the government can think of in order to post all the updates to its partners. They also post the coordinates of the habitatual planets in order for other countries to know where they can send people to. So far, they have big trouble finding people with the talent to navigate spaceships. Pilots are hard to find because of the new and improved ways of flying spaceships using pure brain power, they can try to train but most cadets fail in the first six years of their trainging. They have recruitment fliers in most of the countries.
